3. CONCLUSION

This redesign project reinforced the importance of clarity in digital design. A website or mobile app should communicate its purpose immediately and guide users effortlessly through the experience. Good design is not just about aesthetics—it is about making information easy to understand and actions easy to take.

By restructuring the Jumia landing page, I focused on improving hierarchy, reducing clutter, and highlighting trust elements such as official stores and deals. These changes help users quickly understand what the platform offers and navigate with confidence.

The landing page plays a critical role because it forms the first impression of a brand. When designed effectively, it clearly communicates value, builds trust, and directs users toward meaningful actions. This project strengthened my ability to design with clarity, purpose, and user understanding at the center of every decision.
