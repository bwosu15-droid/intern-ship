PROJECT 1: UX CASE STUDY

APP NAME: APEX (MOBILE APP)

OVERVIEW

Role: UI/UX Designer

Project Type: Conceptual Design

Tools Used: Figma, ChatGPT

Apex is a self-service mobile platform that connects customers with trusted and verified home service professionals such as plumbers, electricians, and maintenance experts. The app is designed to make finding and booking reliable labor services simple, transparent, and stress-free. By combining clear booking systems, real-time availability, and verified service provider profiles, Apex builds trust while giving both clients and professionals full control over their service experience.

1.1 PROBLEM STATEMENT

Finding reliable home service professionals such as plumbers, mechanics, electricians, and housekeepers is often stressful and time-consuming. Customers struggle with unclear availability, inconsistent pricing, lack of trust, and poor communication.

On the other hand, service providers face challenges managing multiple job requests, avoiding overbooking, and organizing their schedules and choosing jobs that fit their availability. The absence of a centralized, transparent platform creates inefficiency, missed opportunities and frustration for both customers and service providers.

1.1.1 SOLUTION

Design a simple and user-friendly mobile application that gives customers clarity while giving service providers better control of bookings.

1.2 GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

  • Make booking simple and fast
  • Reduce user confusion
  • Improve trust and clarity

1.3 CHALLENGES

For Client User:

How to know when to book and which service provider is available and trusted.

For Service Provider User:

How to manage bookings effectively.

1.3.1 SOLUTION

For Client User:

  1. Availability badges: This shows whether a service provider is either Available, Busy or Offline. Green indicates the service provider is available. Yellow indicates the service provider is busy i.e he has been fully booked for the day. However, an option will be provided to give the client an opportunity to book the serviceman for another day. Red indicates the service provider is offline and do not work on said day and such profile will not be accessible.
  2. Verification badges: The app introduces a Verified Badge system for service providers. Service providers complete an ID verification process, including government-issued identification and profile validation. Once approved, a verification badge appears beside their name and profile photo. This badge helps clients quickly identify trusted professionals, builds confidence before booking, and reduces hesitation when allowing service providers into private spaces.

For Service Provider User:

  1. Job offer inbox: Jobs comes as a means of an offer. That is all bookings need to be first confirmed by service provider either by accepting the offer, declining the offer or reschedule the offer.
  2. Auto-reject system: The system auto-rejects other job offers after service provider has been scheduled for the total amount of works he can accept for a day.
  3. Status toggle: Where a service provider is done with the offers he has for the day and can still accept more job offers he can toggle his status from ‘Busy’ to ‘Available’. However will be limited to accepting only one job offer or where he is ‘Offline’ i.e he does not work on a particular he can toggle his status from ‘Offline’ to ‘Available’ giving him the opportunity to work on his or her off day.
