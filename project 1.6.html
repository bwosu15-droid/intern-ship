Apex taught me the true value of designing with real users in mind and testing ideas early before adding polish. A lot of changes were made in getting the desired result, although there can still be improvements. A lot of thought process was put in making sure the problems of the customer/client user was resolved. One thing I took away from this project is that for a UI/UX designer, designing do go beyond pretty screens. What matters is if the app or website meets up with the expectations of the users it is being built for. If an app or website looks pretty but is not usable, clear or understandable what then is the essence of the app or website. Yes, the beauty of the app or website attracts a user but it’s clarity and usability is what keeps the user and also recommend said app or websites to his friends or families with similar issues.
