1. EMPATHIZE

What I did:

  • Conducted informal interviews with students and working professionals who frequently order food.
  • Asked questions about their frustrations when using delivery apps.
  • Observed how users browse and place orders on existing apps.
  • Reviewed user feedback and app store reviews of popular delivery platforms.
  • Created a simple user persona based on common behaviors and pain points.

Key Insights:

  • Many users feel overwhelmed by apps that mix food with groceries and pharmacy items.
  • Users want to find meals quickly without navigating unnecessary categories.
  • Fast checkout and clear pricing are very important.
  • Simplicity increases satisfaction and reduces frustration.
