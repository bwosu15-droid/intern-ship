2. DEFINE

After gathering insights from the Empathize phase, I narrowed down the core user problem and created a realistic user persona to guide my design decisions.

User Persona

Name: Daniel Wilson

Age: 28

Occupation: Sales Manager

Location: Abuja

Tech Comfort Level: Comfortable using mobile apps daily

Background:

Daniel works long hours and often gets home late. His schedule is packed with meetings, client calls, and deadlines. Because of this, he rarely has time to go grocery shopping or cook meals. Most days, he feels too tired to prepare food and prefers a fast, reliable solution for dinner.

He values convenience and efficiency in the apps he uses.

Goals:

  1. Order meals quickly without wasting time.
  2. Avoid grocery shopping after long workdays.
  3. Find quality meals from trusted restaurants.
  4. Repeat previous orders easily.

Needs and Expectations:

  1. A simple and distraction-free interface.
  2. Fast search and filtering options.
  3. Clear pricing with no hidden charges
  4. Accurate delivery time estimates.
  5. Secure and smooth payment process.
  6. Reliable customer support if needed.

Behavior and Usage:

  1. Uses food delivery mostly in the evenings.
  2. Prefers reordering from familiar restaurants.
  3. Makes quick decisions based on ratings and images.
  4. Values speed over exploring too many options.
  5. Uses the app 3–5 times per week.
