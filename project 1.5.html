5. TEST

After designing the high-fidelity prototype, I moved into the test phase to validate whether the solution truly solved the users’ problems.

PROTOTYPE

I created an interactive prototype in Figma and tested it with my brother, my father and a female friend.

Tasks Completed

  1. Create an account and profile.
  2. Book a service.
  3. Navigate screens.

Key Feedback

  1. They were impressed with how easy the app felt in regards to navigation.
  2. They also gave positive remark on the simplicity of the app as it is straightforward and understandable.
  3. They also said the steps in creating an account, creating profile and booking a service were easy.
  4. They also commented on the clarity of the app.
  5. Mostly, they complimented the concept of the app as it is something that will help a lot of people in my country, as it is a common issue when it comes to finding reliable services.
