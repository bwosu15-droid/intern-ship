2. DEFINE

After gathering insights from the Empathize phase, I narrowed down the core user problem and created a realistic user persona to guide my design decisions.

User Persona

Name: Obinna Nwoke

Age: 35

Occupation: Lecturer

Location: Lagos

Tech Comfort Level: Comfortable using mobile apps daily

Background:

Obinna lives in a bungalow and works as a part time lecturer. When things break down at home or he is too busy to get some things done, he struggles to find reliable service providers on short notice. He often relies on word-of-mouth recommendations, which can be unreliable, slow and stressful.

Goals:

  1. Quickly find trusted service providers.
  2. Know availability before requesting a service.
  3. Clearly explain his issue without back and forth calls.
  4. Avoid being overcharged or disappointed.

Frustrations:

  1. Poor communication after initial contact.
  2. Fear of letting unknown workers into his home.
  3. Unreliable artisans who do not show up.
  4. No clarity on pricing or availability.

Needs and Expectations:

  1. Verified servicemen with ratings.
  2. Clear booking status.
  3. Ability to leave detailed notes and photos.
  4. Notifications and updates at every stage.

Behavior and Usage:

  1. Uses his phone to solve problems quickly.
  2. Prefers apps that are simple and clear.
  3. Values transparency and professionalism.
  4. Will abandon apps that feel confusing and unreliable.
