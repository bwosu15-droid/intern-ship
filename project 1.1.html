1. EMPATHIZE

In the empathize phase, I focused on understanding the real challenges in getting trusted and professional servicemen.

What I did:

In this stage I had an interactive session with my dad in regards to the situation of getting trusted professional servicemen. He recalled situations where the family car would breakdown or our house generator breaks down or just situations that would require the services of one professional serviceman or another but had no means of getting in contact with one or the ones he knew would either be unavailable or would make promises to come and fail to come with no prior notice of their absence.

Key Insights:

  • Broken promises of servicemen to come on agreed day and time.
  • No definite place to check available servicemen.
  • Relying on recommendations by friends.
  • Lack of trust as there is no means of verifying servicemen.
