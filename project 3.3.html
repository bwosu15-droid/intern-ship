3. IDEATE

After defining the core problem and understanding Daniel’s needs, I moved into the ideate phase. This phase focused on exploring multiple ideas and narrowing them down to practical, user-centered solutions.

USER-FLOW

(Onboarding - Registration [First time user])

→

Step 1

After installing and opening the app, the user arrives at the welcome screen which after a 3 seconds or with a tap on the screen goes to the next screen.

→

Step 2

The user is taken to the intro screen which is 3 in number. The user clicks the ‘right arrow’ button which takes them through the intro screens. On the last screen, the user clicks the ‘get started’ button to sign up.

Step 3

After clicking sign up, the user can register with either his Email, Google account or Apple account.

→

Step 4

After registration, the user is taken to the next screen where they fill out a basic profile information and also upload a profile picture (optional). The information includes their Name and Phone number.

→

Step 5

After filling out the information. The user clicks on the ‘continue’ button which takes them to the next screen where the put their address either manually or through live location which automatically brings out their address.

Step 6

After putting their address which is their delivery address the user is taken to the home screen..

USER-FLOW

(Placing Order for Meal [Through All Restaurant’s section] )

→

Step 1

The user clicks on their favorite restaurant on the home screen and is taken to the restaurants page.

→

Step 2

On the restaurants page, user clicks on meal of choice and is taken to the meals screen.

Step 3

On the meals screen, the user can select quantity of meal and add to cart. A “pick preferences” option is also available for restaurants that allows customers to make preferences to their meals.

→

Step 4

After the user directly adds meal to cart or make preferences to his meal before adding to cart, the user clicks on the cart icon and is taken to ‘my cart’ screen.

→

Step 5

On the ‘my cart’ screen, users can add vouchers if they have any or adjust quantity of meals then proceeds to the checkout.

Step 6

On the checkout screen, the user can select their method of payment. User can also edit their delivery address if necessary. After selecting payment method, they can proceed to make payment.

→

Step 7

On the make payment screen, depending on the payment method selected, user will need to fill necessary details to make payment. User can also click on the back arrow icon to select different payment method. An option is also provided to make first payment method as default payment method. After filing details, the user confirms payment.

Step 8

After confirming payment, the user receives information that the order is confirmed and meal is being prepared. The user is also given the option to download receipt. If not user can click on the ‘track order’ button to track meal progress or click on home icon to go to home screen.
