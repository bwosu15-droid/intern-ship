1. DESIGN STRATEGY

To address these issues, I adopted a user-centered design approach focused on clarity, trust, and simplicity.

Designs decisions included:

i. Introducing a clean hero section with a strong value proposition.

ii. Structuring the landing page into clear, purposeful sections.

iii. Creating a dedicated section for official brand stores.

iv. Highlighting deals using consistent card layouts.

v. Adding testimonials to provide social proof and reassurance.
