5. TEST

After designing the high-fidelity prototype, I moved into the test phase to validate whether the solution truly solved the users’ problems.

PROTOTYPE

I created an interactive prototype in figma and tested it with two of my male friends.

Tasks Completed

  1. Create an account and profile.
  2. Order a meal.
  3. Navigate screens.

Key Feedback

  1. They liked that it focused only on food and was not cluttered with groceries or other services.
  2. They found it easy to move between Home, Search, Orders, and Profile without confusion.
  3. They liked the large food images and clear pricing.
  4. They said the checkout process was straightforward and not overwhelming.
  5. They said they app felt clean and easy to understand.
