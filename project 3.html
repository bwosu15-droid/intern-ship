PROJECT 3: UX CASE STUDY

APP NAME: EATZY (MOBILE APP)

OVERVIEW

Role: UI/UX Designer

Project Type: Concept Design

Tools Used: Figma, ChatGPT

Eatzy is a food-only delivery app designed to simplify the ordering experience. Unlike multi-service platforms that deliver groceries, drugs, and other items, Eatzy focuses strictly on food — making the experience faster, cleaner, and easier for users.

1.1 PROBLEM STATEMENT

Many delivery apps combine food, groceries, pharmacy items, and other services into one platform. While this offers variety, it often results in cluttered interfaces, overwhelming choices, and slower navigation.

Users who simply want to order food are forced to scroll through unrelated categories, making the experience less efficient and more frustrating. There is a need for a focused, food-only delivery app that simplifies the process and makes ordering meals quick and seamless.

1.1.1 SOLUTION

I designed a food-only delivery app focused strictly on meals and restaurants.

The solution includes:

  1. Simple navigation (Home, Search, Orders, Profile).
  2. Clear food categories.
  3. Large food images for easy browsing.
  4. Fast and minimal checkout process.

This creates a clean, distraction-free ordering experience.

1.2 GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

  1. Improve ordering speed
  2. Reduce user confusion
  3. Make food discovery easy

1.3 CHALLENGES

  1. Keeping the design minimal but not boring.
  2. Standing out from multi-service apps.
  3. Organizing food categories clearly.
  4. Designing for quick decision-making.
